Did You Know?
  • The average American produces about 16 tons of CO2 per year
  • Globally, we need to reach 2 tons per person by 2050 to avoid severe climate change
  • Simple changes like eating less meat and driving less can significantly reduce your footprint
  • Reducing your carbon footprint often saves money too, through lower energy bills and reduced consumption
